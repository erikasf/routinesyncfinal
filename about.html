Patient Advocacy Through Technology

We at mendux, tackle problems from creative out of the box ideas to find the solutions to some of healthcares biggest problems. These ideas and solutions are a product of directly working "at the bedsided" with our clients and patients

Each one of our team members has had a point in their life where medical care or assistive technology that was needed for either themselveds or their loved ones, was out of their budget. We don't think that is fair, so for ever dollar that we take in a percentage is put aside in a fund to help those that can't afford it right now have access.
